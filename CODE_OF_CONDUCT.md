# Code of conduct

Discussion should stay specific to AI production readiness and the published curation rules.

Participants should:

- challenge entries and descriptions with evidence;
- disclose affiliation with a proposed resource;
- avoid harassment and personal attacks;
- avoid publishing secrets, personal data, or confidential material;
- distinguish a live link from a maintained, suitable, or secure project;
- respect third-party licenses and upstream disclosure processes.

The maintainer may remove comments, issues, or pull requests that violate these expectations.
